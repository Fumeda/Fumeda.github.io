How do I start brewing?

Before you can get a potion, you need to charge your alchemy kit with stims

Stims give differing amounts of charge. The 'stronger' a stim is, the more it'll give
note: there currently is a bug where the first stim added gives 1% less charge than it's supposed to

Table of stim charge values:
Hope 75%
Confidence 65%
Necro 50%
Neutriliagen 35%
AL27-Q9 35%
K3JB-4E 30%
H14-B 30%
Adrenaline 25%

How do I get a certain type of potion?

Each button you press adds a score value

Button press order has no significance
e.g AWA, AAW, WAA all result in the same potion (with a total score of 5, which is a harming potion)

Depending on the total amount of score, your potion will be a different type

All types are listed below, along with their required score values and effects
